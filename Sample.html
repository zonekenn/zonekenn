WHAT IS COMPUTER ENGINEERING?

Computer Engineering is a dynamic and ever-evolving field that sits at the intersection of computer science and electrical engineering. It encompasses a wide range of topics related to the design, development, and maintenance of computer systems and their components. In this comprehensive overview, we will delve into the fascinating world of computer engineering, exploring its core principles, applications, and the exciting possibilities it offers.

Mission And Vision

Our Mission

    Since 1999, COESS has been committed in the mission of helping the College and the University in producing skilled and competitive Computer Engineers by providing programs and activities that will boost their knowledge and specialization in the areas of computer system design, software engineering, computer networks, embedded systems, instrumentation, and control, etc.

Our Vision

As a recognized student organization, our objective is to develop a camaraderie among Computer Engineering Students in the University, producing a portrayal of good character, excellency, productive computer engineer. Molding them to be the leader of our future.
